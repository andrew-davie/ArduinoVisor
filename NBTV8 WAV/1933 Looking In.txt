The First Television Revue
'Looking In', a live revue
broadcast by the BBC on
21st April 1933. The show
starts with the Paramount
Astoria dancing girls and
was half an hour long. The
surviving footage was
recorded by a home viewer
onto a Silvatone 78 rpm
aluminium disk.
