Phonovision Experiment
This is one of the
earliest of the records
made by Baird, recorded
10th January 1928.
The Phonovision 78 rpm
disc resembled a 10-inch
diameter wax audio disc,
lasting ~3 minutes. The
subject on this disc is
possibly Wally Fowlkes.
